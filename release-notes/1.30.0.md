# Forge Method 1.30.0

Guided human experience.

## Highlights

- Broad first-run ideas now start with a full brain dump before artifacts narrow the product.
- The opening facilitation now asks what else is still in the human's head, what the product must not become, and whether the human wants a fast path with assumptions or step-by-step coaching.
- Guidance Engine now exposes observable pace contracts: `coaching`, `fast-path`, `diagnostic`, `divergent`, `evidence-first`, `repair`, and `mechanical`.
- No-state routing now sends confused users to problem-solving, explicit brainstorms to divergent facilitation, research requests to evidence-first scans, and frustrated guidance feedback to correct-course.
- Stress coverage now exercises broad game ideas, rushed/simple requests, lost users, brainstorm, research, drift, and frustrated/cold guidance against both source and installed-plugin behavior.
- Focused verification loops are documented for short development checks without replacing the broader runtime and install validation gates.
