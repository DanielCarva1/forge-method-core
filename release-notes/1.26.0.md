# Forge Method 1.26.0

Forge Method 1.26 improves first-run human experience and adds a canonical Reality/Evidence Gate.

## Highlights

- Empty and brownfield work now starts with a concise human explanation before technical state details.
- Non-JSON guidance can be warmer, more direct, and more opinionated while JSON/state/evidence stay compact.
- `preflight --json` and `guide --json` now expose compact `human_experience` and `reality_evidence_gate` payloads.
- Reality/Evidence Gate blocks impossible, cruel, unsafe, illegal, or incoherent ideas before market claims.
- Market, Domain, and Technical Feasibility scan workflows are packaged for discovery and planning.

## Notes

`$forge-method` remains the only public entrypoint. The new scans are internal Forge workflows for agents to load when discovery needs them.
